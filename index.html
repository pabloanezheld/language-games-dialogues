About Colombia

Colombia sits at the nexus of Caribbean and Pacific coastlines, Andean highlands, and Amazon rainforest. Expect warm hospitality, diverse ecosystems, lively music, and exceptional food and coffee.

Top Destinations

Cartagena

Colorful colonial streets and Caribbean beaches — perfect for history and sunsets.

Medellín

City of eternal spring — innovation, nightlife, and lush surrounding valleys.

Bogotá

High-altitude capital with museums, street art, and a thriving food scene.

Tayrona Park

Caribbean rainforest meets turquoise waters — hiking and pristine beaches.

Coffee Region

Rolling hills, coffee tours, and charming towns like Salento and Filandia.

Practical Info

  • Currency: Colombian Peso (COP)
  • Language: Spanish (English commonly spoken in tourist areas)
  • Best time: Varies by region — generally December–March for Caribbean coast; July–August good for many inland areas.
  • Health & Safety: Use common-sense precautions; travel insurance recommended.

Travel Tips

  1. Try local specialties: arepas, bandeja paisa, fresh ceviche, and coffee tastings.
  2. Use day tours for remote areas and hire local guides when visiting parks.
  3. Pack for heat on the coast and layers for Andean evenings.
